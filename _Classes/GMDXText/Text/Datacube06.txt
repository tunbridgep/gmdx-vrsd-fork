<P>DEFAULT WEAPON CONTROLS
<P>
<P>Shoot: Left Mouse Button
<P>Holster: Right Mouse Button
<P>Reload: R
<P>Change Weapon: Mouse Wheel
<P>Drop Weapon: J
<P>Change Ammo Type: X
<P>Toggle Scope: Middle Mouse
<P>Toggle Laser Sight: Z
<P>
<P>Advanced Controls:
<P>Scope Zoom In/Out: Mouse Wheel (while scope is active)
<P>Use Secondary Weapon: F (if you have a Secondary Weapon)
<P>Cancel Reload: Left Mouse Button (while reloading. Shotguns/crossbow only)
<P>
<P>You can rebind keys at any time from the 'Keyboard/Mouse' menu.
